SongKang

Song Kang (Korean: 송강; born April 23, 1994) is a South Korean actor. His notable lead roles in television series include Love Alarm (2019–2021), Sweet Home (2020-2024), Navillera (2021), Nevertheless (2021), Forecasting Love and Weather (2022), and My Demon (2023). He is popularly known as the "Son of Netflix" because most of his series are aired on the platform.
